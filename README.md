# Em dash highlighter

The em dash is the powerhouse of the written word—it adds drama, creates pause, and structures complex thoughts. Yet, in a sea of standard text, these stylistic heroes often go unnoticed.

Em Dash Highlighter transforms your browser into a precision editing suite by instantly isolating the most powerful punctuation mark in the English language. With a striking high-contrast white-on-red overlay, it eliminates visual clutter and ensures you never lose track of your narrative flow or structural pauses again.
